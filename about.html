About us!

Solarpaint wants to turn any building surface into any energy with a simple coat of paint. To meet the growing demand for clean renewable energy by creating a new affordable solar solution that can be deployed on a global scale. Solar paint solution is an innovation effort to meet UNs sustainable development goal 7; to Ensure access to affordable, reliable, sustainable and modern energy.

Today, Solarpaint is looking for partners who want to give cities and urban areas clean renewable energy with a fresh coat of paint. The Solarpaint tema consists of a diverse team of people from all over the world to help us understand and develop local energy solutions. From remote villages to large metropolitan areas our solar technology can be scaled to meet both low and high scale demands.
